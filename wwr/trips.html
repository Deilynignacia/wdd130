The Eclipse Trial Limited!

Only offered during full moons. An exclusive night expedition offered only under the ethereal veil of a partial or total solar eclipse. This is more than a rafting trip; it's a cosmic odyssey designed to mirror the celestial event above.

This is an unparalleled opportunity to face the unknown in the heart of the night, to find clarity in the penumbra, and to emerge transformed, your spirit aligned with the grand design of the cosmos. Embrace the power of the eclipse and write a chapter of your legend under the celestial spectacle.

All necessary equipment for safe navigation and specialized instruments for enhanced visibility under the cosmic veil will be provided by Destiny's Wake.
